attack chain: browser-in-the-middle fullscreen overlay

Attackers trigger fullscreen mode (often without user gesture via JavaScript), then render a fake browser chrome overlay with a spoofed URL bar showing https://accounts.google.com. The victim believes they're on the legitimate site and enters credentials into the attacker-controlled form. Safari remains unpatched. 1Password ≤8.11.27.2 and LastPass ≤4.150.1 are vulnerable.
