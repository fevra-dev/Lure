attack chain: toad screen share

Attacker calls victim impersonating IT support, directs them to a web page, and requests screen share via getDisplayMedia(). Once sharing is active, the attacker observes MFA codes, password entry, sensitive documents, and session tokens. Used by MuddyWater/TA450, RansomHub, Luna Moth, and BazarCall. TOAD represents 47% of documented 2025 social engineering attacks.
