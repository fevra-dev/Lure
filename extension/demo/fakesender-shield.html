attack chain: helpdesk platform brand impersonation

Attacker creates a free-tier helpdesk account (e.g., coinbase-help.zendesk.com) with a brand display name. Emails sent from the platform pass SPF/DKIM/DMARC because they originate from legitimate helpdesk infrastructure. Victims land on the helpdesk subdomain which impersonates the brand and may contain credential harvesting forms or links to external phishing pages.
