attack chain: qrljacking session hijack

Attackers create a phishing page that continuously proxies a legitimate service's QR code (WhatsApp Web, Teams, Entra ID). The QR refreshes every 2–3 seconds to stay synchronized. When the victim scans the cloned QR, they authenticate the attacker's session. Cofense reports 587% quishing increase (Q1 2024 vs Q1 2023). Storm-2372, APT29, and TA2723 all use QR + Device Code Flow delivery.
